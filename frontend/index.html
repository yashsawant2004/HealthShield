About HealthShield

HealthShield is an independent health insurance comparison and advisory platform founded with one mission: to make it simple for every Indian family to find the right health cover without confusion, hidden commissions, or insurer bias.

We aggregate policy data from 20+ IRDAI-registered insurers, apply a transparent scoring algorithm, and surface the policies that genuinely match your health profile, family situation, and budget. We are not an insurance company; we are your navigator.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this service free?
Yes, completely free. We earn no commissions from any insurer for showing you their policies.
Are the recommendations unbiased?
Fully. Our scoring algorithm is rule-based and driven purely by your profile vs policy features — no paid placements.
Can I buy a policy here?
Not yet — we are a comparison tool. Download the policy brochure and contact the insurer directly to purchase.
How current is the policy data?
We refresh the catalog regularly. Always verify terms with the insurer's official prospectus before purchasing.
Is my personal data stored?
No personal data is stored or shared. Your profile inputs are only used in-session for recommendations.
What is a top-up policy?
A top-up policy activates after your base policy's sum insured is exhausted, providing additional cover at a lower premium than enhancing the base policy.
